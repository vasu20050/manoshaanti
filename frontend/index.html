Privacy & Precautions

Your data, your safety, and our commitment to you

Data Privacy & Storage

At ManoShaanti, your privacy is our top priority. We understand that mental health is a deeply personal subject, and we handle your data accordingly:

  • Local Storage: Currently, all your chats, mood entries, and assessment scores are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal data on external servers.
  • Confidentiality: Your conversations with the AI companion are strictly confidential and are not shared with any third parties or human reviewers.
  • Your Control: Since your data is saved in your browser, clearing your browser data or local storage will completely erase your history.

AI Companion Precautions

While our AI is designed to be empathetic and supportive, it is essential to understand its limitations:

  • Not a Doctor: The AI companion is a supportive tool, not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  • No PII: Please avoid sharing Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your full name, address, social security numbers, or financial information in the chat.
  • Limitations: The AI may sometimes provide general advice that may not perfectly fit your unique situation. Always consult a licensed professional for severe concerns.

Safety & Emergency Protocol

ManoShaanti is a wellness tool and is not equipped to handle medical emergencies or crisis situations.

If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, please immediately:
  • Call your local emergency services (e.g., 911)
  • Call or text the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline at 988 (in the US)
  • Go to the nearest hospital emergency room
